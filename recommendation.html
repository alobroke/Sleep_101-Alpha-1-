Personalized Sleep Recommendations

General Tips:

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends.
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals at least 4 hours before bedtime.
  • Engage in regular physical activity, but not too close to bedtime.
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine (reading, warm bath, etc.).
  • Reduce screen time 1 hour before bed to improve melatonin production.
  • Keep your sleeping environment cool, dark, and quiet.

Suggestions for High Stress or Low Activity:

  • Try mindfulness meditation or journaling.
  • Increase physical activity to at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Use progressive muscle relaxation before bed.
  • Track your sleep using a journal or app.
